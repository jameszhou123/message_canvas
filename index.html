Message Canvas Privacy Policy

Message Canvas is a user-friendly app designed to help you create full-screen text displays with customizable backgrounds and text styles. Our app is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled responsibly.

Data Collection and Usage

Message Canvas does not collect any user data. Photos taken using the camera feature within the app are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted or stored on any external servers.

Permissions

Our app requires access to your device's camera to allow you to take photos and use them as background images for your text displays. The camera access is solely used for this purpose and does not involve collecting, transmitting, or storing any personal information.

Children's Privacy

Message Canvas is designed for users of all ages and does not collect any personal information from users. As a result, our app complies with children's privacy laws, such as the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) in the United States.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update our privacy policy from time to time. Any changes to the policy will be reflected on this page. We encourage users to check this page periodically for updates.
